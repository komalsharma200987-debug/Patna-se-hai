# Patna Tourism Website

Welcome to the official Patna Tourism Website! This professional website showcases the rich cultural heritage, sacred temples, famous attractions, and delicious cuisine of Patna, Bihar.

## Created By
**Mayank Kumar**

## Overview

This website is designed to celebrate and promote tourism in Patna, one of the oldest inhabited cities in the world. It features:

### 🏛️ Sacred Temples
- **Hanuman Mandir** - A place of devotion and spiritual awakening
- **Sheetla Mandir** - Known for its healing powers
- **Mahavir Mandir** - Important Jain temple with magnificent architecture
- **Kali Mandir** - Sacred temple of Goddess Kali
- **Golghar** - Historic golden dome with panoramic city views
- **Patna Sahib Gurudwara** - Birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh

### 🏞️ Famous Places to Visit
- **Patna Zoo** - One of India's oldest zoos with diverse wildlife
- **Gandhi Maidan** - Historic public ground and spacious park
- **Nalanda University Ruins** - UNESCO World Heritage Site
- **Patna Sahib Area** - Sacred pilgrimage destination
- **Birla Planetarium** - Space and astronomy experiences
- **Kumhrar Archaeological Site** - Ancient Mauryan Empire remnants

### 🍜 Culinary Delights
- Litti Chokha - Traditional Bihari specialty
- Pani Puri - Popular street food
- Sattu Paratha - Nutritious flatbread
- Dalpuri - Lentil-filled flatbread
- Chikhalwali - Traditional sweet
- Khichdi & Aloo Bhate - Comfort food
- Til Barfi - Sesame brittle candy
- Malpua - Sweet pancakes with rabri
