About Patna

Patna, the capital of Bihar, is one of the oldest inhabited cities in the world. With a history spanning thousands of years, Patna has been home to empires, saints, and spiritual leaders.

Created by: Mayank Kumar

This website celebrates the rich cultural heritage, sacred temples, delicious cuisine, and breathtaking attractions that make Patna truly special.

Sacred Temples of Patna

Hanuman Mandir

One of the most revered temples dedicated to Lord Hanuman. A place of devotion and spiritual awakening for thousands of devotees.

  • Gyan Vihar, Patna
  • 6 AM - 9 PM

Sheetla Mandir

Dedicated to Goddess Sheetla, this ancient temple is known for its healing powers and attracts devotees throughout the year.

  • Patna City
  • 5 AM - 10 PM

Mahavir Mandir

One of the most important Jain temples, dedicated to Lord Mahavira. A masterpiece of religious architecture and spirituality.

  • Patna City
  • 6 AM - 8 PM

Kali Mandir

Sacred temple of Goddess Kali offering spiritual solace and divine blessings to all who visit with a pure heart.

  • Patna
  • 6 AM - 9 PM

Golghar (Golden Dome)

A historic grain storage structure with beautiful architecture. Offers panoramic views of Patna and the Ganges River.

  • Patna Sahib
  • 6 AM - 6 PM

Patna Sahib Gurudwara

One of the most sacred Sikh pilgrimage sites, birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh. A place of immense spiritual significance.

  • Patna Sahib
  • 4 AM - 10 PM

Famous Places to Visit

Patna Zoo

One of India's oldest zoos with diverse wildlife. Home to lions, tigers, elephants, and many exotic species. Perfect for family visits.

Patna Affordable Entry

Gandhi Maidan

Historic public ground where freedom fighters gathered. Now a spacious park perfect for evening walks and cultural events.

Central Patna Public Space

Nalanda University Ruins

UNESCO World Heritage Site of one of the world's oldest universities. Witness the architectural grandeur of ancient Buddhist learning.

Near Patna UNESCO Heritage

Patna Sahib Area

Sacred area famous for the historic Gurudwara and the birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh. Spiritually enriching destination.

Patna Sahib Pilgrimage Site

Birla Planetarium

Experience the wonders of space and astronomy. Educational and entertaining shows for all age groups.

Patna Science Attraction

Kumhrar Archaeological Site

Ancient site with remnants of Mauryan Empire. Fascinating insights into Patna's glorious past and historical significance.

Culinary Delights of Patna

Experience the authentic flavors and traditional cuisine of Bihar

Litti Chokha

Baked gram flour balls served with mashed vegetables. A traditional Bihari delicacy that's both filling and delicious.

Traditional Bihari

Pani Puri

Crispy puri shells filled with spiced potatoes and chickpeas, served with tangy tamarind water. A street food favorite.

Street Food

Sattu Paratha

Roasted gram flour stuffed flatbread. Nutritious and energizing, perfect for breakfast or lunch.

Traditional Bihari

Dalpuri

Deep-fried flatbread filled with roasted lentils. Served with aloo bhate and pickle for an authentic Bihari meal.

Traditional Bihari

Chikhalwali (Sweet)

Traditional sweet delicacy made with jaggery and gram flour. A festive favorite during celebrations.

Traditional Sweet

Khichdi & Aloo Bhate

Simple yet comforting rice and lentil dish served with spiced potato curry. A comfort food staple.

Comfort Food

Til Barfi

Sesame brittle candy made with jaggery. A popular winter sweet and perfect as a souvenir.

Traditional Sweet

Malpua

Sweet pancakes soaked in sugar syrup and topped with rabri. A dessert favorite at celebrations.
